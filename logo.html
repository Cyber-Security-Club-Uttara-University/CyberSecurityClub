Clear Space & Minimum Size

Proper spacing ensures the logo remains clear and impactful. Never compromise on these minimum requirements.

Important Spacing Rule

Always maintain adequate clear space around the logo. The minimum clear space should be equal to the height of the "C" in CSC.

Minimum Width

Print: 25mm / 1 inch
Digital: 150px

Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of "C" on all sides

Do's and Don'ts

Follow these essential rules to maintain logo integrity and brand consistency across all applications.

Do's

  • Use official logo files
  • Maintain aspect ratio
  • Use on contrasting backgrounds
  • Keep adequate clear space
  • Use approved colors

Don'ts

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change logo colors
  • Add effects or shadows
  • Rotate the logo
  • Place on busy backgrounds

Detailed Usage Guidelines

Comprehensive guidelines for using our logo across different mediums and platforms.

Color Usage

Use the white logo on dark backgrounds and the blue logo on light backgrounds. Ensure sufficient contrast for readability.

File Formats

Use PNG for digital applications with transparency. For print, request vector files (SVG/EPS) for best quality.

Print Quality

Ensure logo is at least 300 DPI for print materials. Never upscale low-resolution versions.

Digital Usage

For web and mobile, use PNG format. Ensure logo is clearly visible at all screen sizes.

Prohibited Uses

  • Do not use for commercial purposes without permission
  • Do not modify or alter the logo
  • Do not use as part of another logo

Brand Protection

The CSC logo is a registered trademark. Use only with proper authorization and in accordance with these guidelines.
