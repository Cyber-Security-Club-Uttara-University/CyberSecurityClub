2. Registration & Payment

2.1 Registration Requirements

  • All participants must register using their valid Uttara University email address (@uttara.ac.bd or @uttarauniversity.edu.bd)
  • Accurate personal information must be provided during registration
  • Each student is permitted only one registration per academic ID
  • Registration is non-transferable between individuals

2.2 Payment Terms

  • Registration fee: 200 BDT (Bangladeshi Taka) for student pass
  • Payment must be completed via bKash, Rocket, or Nagad to the designated number: 01919399235
  • Valid transaction ID must be provided during registration
  • Pre-booked participants are exempt from payment
  • All payments are subject to verification by the organizing committee

2.3 Refund Policy

No Refund Policy: All registration fees are non-refundable once payment is confirmed. This includes cases of cancellation, no-show, or withdrawal for any reason.

3. Event Access & Participation

3.1 Entry Requirements

  • Valid ticket ID and student identification must be presented at the venue
  • Entry is granted on a first-come, first-served basis within capacity limits
  • Late arrivals may not be guaranteed entry if capacity is reached
  • Re-entry policies will be determined by venue management on event day

3.2 Included Benefits

Your student pass includes:

  • Access to all keynote talks and speaker sessions
  • Participation in CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions
  • Entry to interactive villages and workshops
  • Breakfast and lunch on event day
  • Official CyberCon25 swag
  • Workshop materials and resources
  • Certificate of participation
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals

4. Code of Conduct

4.1 Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

  • Treat all attendees, speakers, sponsors, and staff with respect and courtesy
  • Engage professionally and constructively in all sessions and activities
  • Respect intellectual property and confidentiality
  • Follow all instructions from event organizers and security personnel
  • Maintain the integrity of the event venue and equipment

4.2 Prohibited Conduct

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
  • Unauthorized recording or photography during restricted sessions
  • Malicious hacking or unauthorized access to event systems
  • Possession or consumption of alcohol or illegal substances
  • Disruptive behavior during presentations or workshops
  • Unauthorized commercial activities or solicitation
  • Theft, vandalism, or damage to property

4.3 Consequences

Violation of the code of conduct may result in:

  • Warning from event organizers
  • Immediate expulsion from the event without refund
  • Ban from future Cyber Security Club events
  • Reporting to university authorities
  • Legal action if applicable
