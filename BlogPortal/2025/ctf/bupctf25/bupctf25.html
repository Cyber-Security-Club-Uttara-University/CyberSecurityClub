UUCSC Teams Rocked BUP CSE Tech Carnival 2025

Shout-out to Our Amazing Teams! Our Cyber Security Club, Uttara University, rocked the BUP CSE Tech Carnival 2025 – BUP CTF Powered by Knight Squad!

About the Competition

BUP CSE Tech Carnival 2025 brought together Bangladesh's finest cybersecurity enthusiasts for an intense CTF competition powered by Knight Squad. Our teams represented Uttara University with exceptional skill, dedication, and passion throughout the final round.

Final Round Results

  • NullSec Ninjas - Secured 11th Place
  • Team Hackalypse - Secured 20th Place
  • Kernel Panic - Secured 33rd Place

From preparation to performance, these teams have shown what dedication and passion can achieve. Their commitment, teamwork, and enthusiasm throughout the competition truly reflect the spirit of our club — learn, practice, and grow together.

The CSC Spirit

At Cyber Security Club, we believe in fostering a collaborative learning environment where members can:

  • Learn - Continuously expand knowledge in cybersecurity domains
  • Practice - Apply theoretical concepts to real-world challenges
  • Grow Together - Support each other's journey in the field

What This Means for Us

Participating in prestigious competitions like BUP CTF is a testament to our members' dedication and the quality of training provided by the Cyber Security Club. These experiences are invaluable for:

  • Building practical cybersecurity skills
  • Understanding real-world security scenarios
  • Developing teamwork and collaboration
  • Networking with fellow security enthusiasts
  • Preparing for professional careers in cybersecurity

Looking Forward

We congratulate our members for their dedication and wish them continued success in future competitions!

This competition has not only tested our teams' technical abilities but has also strengthened their resolve to keep learning and improving. We look forward to seeing our members participate in more such events and continue to make Uttara University proud.
