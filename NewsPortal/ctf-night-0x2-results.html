About CTF Night

CTF Night is a monthly Capture The Flag competition hosted by Cyber Security Club, Uttara University. These monthly solo events are designed to provide engaging practice opportunities for students interested in cybersecurity. Each CTF Night features beginner to intermediate-level challenges that help participants develop practical skills in a competitive environment.

What's Next?

Stay tuned for upcoming CTF events! Follow our social media channels and check the CTF page regularly for announcements about the next CTF Night and other competitions.
