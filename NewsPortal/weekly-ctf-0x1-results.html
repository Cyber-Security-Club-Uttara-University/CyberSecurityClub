About Weekly CTF

Weekly CTF competitions are designed to provide regular practice opportunities for students interested in cybersecurity. These events feature beginner to intermediate-level challenges across various categories including web exploitation, cryptography, forensics, OSINT, and more.

What's Next?

Stay tuned for upcoming CTF events! Follow our social media channels and check the CTF page regularly for announcements about the next Weekly CTF and Monthly CTF competitions.
